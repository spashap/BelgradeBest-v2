## 1. The theme and organising idea (stable concept)

[CONFIRMED] The official theme is "Play for Humanity: Sport and Music for All." The programme explores how play — through sport and music — builds resilience, creativity, connection and well-being.
(Source: Expo 2027 official About page; official Programme page, accessed 4 June 2026)

[CONFIRMED] The official programme is structured into three subthemes, each explored through a curated series of events and experiences that guide the flow of the visit.
(Source: Expo 2027 official Programme page, accessed 4 June 2026)

[CONFIRMED] The Expo runs 15 May–15 August 2027 (93 days), and the programme is framed around that full three-month span.
(Source: Expo 2027 official Programme page)

---

## 2. The thematic pavilions and Forum (the on-site structure)

[CONFIRMED] The official concept describes three thematic pavilions plus a Forum:
 – The Power of Play Pavilion — the science and psychology of play.
 – The Play for Progress Pavilion — innovation and technological breakthroughs driven by playful exploration.
 – The Play Together Pavilion — sport, music and collaborative play as tools for social connection.
 – The Forum — talks, panels, workshops and networking on play's role in society.
(Source: Expo 2027 official About page)

[CONFIRMED] Highlighted thematic programmes include "Music of all for all" (world music performances, workshops, sonic innovation), "Sport of all for all" (global sports events and ambassadors of play), and "Limitless Play" (record-breaking challenges, collaborative feats, interactive competitions).
(Source: Expo 2027 official About page)

[CONFIRMED] City-wide "Playgrounds" are planned as public-space interventions beyond the main site: "Playing in Between" (relaxation and spontaneous play zones), "Generational playgrounds" (intergenerational play areas) and "Playground Earth" (global sustainability-themed installations).
(Source: Expo 2027 official About page)

## 3. National Days and country programming

[CONFIRMED] Each participating country is expected to have a designated National Day to present its culture, innovation and programme.

[REPORTED] Independent guidance frames National Days as the most atmospheric days to visit — full cultural programme, food and performances — though which country falls on which date is not yet public.

[UNKNOWN] The schedule of National Days (country-to-date mapping) has not been published.

## 4. Scale and brand ambassadors

[REPORTED] The Expo is described as featuring more than 8,000 events across its 93 days.
(Source: beinbelgrade.com consolidation, Sept 2025 — non-official)

[CONFIRMED] Expo 2027 has appointed global brand ambassadors to carry the "Play for Humanity" message: Olympic sprint champion Usain Bolt (the first ambassador) and film star Jackie Chan, with organisers stating more figures will join. Their pairing is framed as symbolising the sport-and-music pillars.
(Source: Expo 2027 official news "Brand Ambassadors… Jackie Chan," 2025; Euronews Serbia, Sept 2025; Bernama/Xinhua, Sept–Oct 2025)

[REPORTED] The ambassadors were engaged for unspecified fees and presented publicly by the Serbian President; the Expo's broader marketing has drawn domestic political criticism (state-funded promotion amid anti-government protests). Include only if the article addresses context/controversy; attribute clearly.
(Source: Balkan Insight, 18 Sept 2025)
