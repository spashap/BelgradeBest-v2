## How to Use This Knowledge Base

Use this document as a **source-controlled article planning base**, not as final article copy.

### Fact labels

- **[CONFIRMED]** = stated by official Expo / BIE / Serbian government source, or directly verifiable from a primary source.
- **[REPORTED]** = reported by media, business press, expert commentary, or secondary sources; useful but should be rechecked before publishing.
- **[UNKNOWN/TBD]** = not publicly confirmed yet, or likely to change.
- **[CONFLICT FLAG]** = sources give different numbers or definitions; article copy must explain the conflict clearly.

### Best publishing rule

When writing public articles, prefer this hierarchy:

1. **Official Expo 2027 Belgrade website** — primary for theme, dates, participants, programme pages, tickets when published.
2. **BIE / Planet Expo** — primary for Expo classification, recognition, official status.
3. **Serbian government / official agencies** — primary for infrastructure, security, state plans.
4. **Official participant-country government pages** — best source for national pavilion plans.
5. **Business press / media / expert commentary** — useful for investment, hotels, real estate, controversy, market analysis.

# 5. Editorial Warnings Before Publishing

## Numbers that must be checked before every article update

1. Number of participating countries.
2. Ticket prices and sales opening date.
3. Daily event calendar / national days.
4. Railway completion date and actual operating route.
5. Hotel capacity and new hotel openings.
6. Visitor projection language — “visitors” vs “visits” may not mean the same thing.
7. Site size — distinguish **core Expo site**, **logistics zone**, and **wider Surčin complex**.

## Never write as final fact yet

- “Tickets cost X” — no official prices yet.
- “The railway will definitely open on X date” — planned but construction/timing risk.
- “All 137 countries are listed publicly” — official page says 137 but public list may not show all in a clean table.
- “4.1 million unique visitors” — some sources refer to visits; treat as projected attendance/visits unless clarified.
- “Post-Expo legacy is guaranteed exactly as described” — current material is a plan/intention.
